How FoodBridge Works

A smart platform powered by AI to reduce food waste and feed communities

Donor Module

Restaurants, hotels, and caterers upload surplus food, set pickup times, and track their donation impact.

NGO Module

View nearby food donations, accept requests, and manage storage capacity for efficient redistribution.

Volunteer Module

Receive pickup notifications, navigate to donor locations, and deliver food to NGOs and shelters.

AI Prediction

Machine learning predicts surplus food quantities, reducing waste and enabling proactive coordination.

Smart Matching

Automatically finds the nearest NGO based on proximity, capacity, and food type compatibility.

Impact Dashboard

Real-time analytics showing meals distributed, food waste reduced, active donors, and CO₂ saved.
